About Me

Researcher & Educator

Hello! I'm Furqan Rustam, a researcher and educator specialising in Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence. Originally from Pakistan, my work is driven by a passion for building intelligent systems that make the digital world safer.

I am pursuing my Ph.D. at University College Dublin, where my research applies Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing to detect and mitigate harmful online interactions.

Alongside research, I teach and mentor students across CS, AI, and Data Science, and freelance on Fiverr as a Level 2 Seller delivering machine learning and technical writing projects.

I'm always open to collaboration on projects aimed at making digital environments more secure, trustworthy, and resilient.
